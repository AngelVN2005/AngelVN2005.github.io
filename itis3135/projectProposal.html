Wendy's Kitchen

Project Overview

  • Purpuse

    To provide customers with an easy-to-use online ordering experience for Wendy's Kitchen, allowing them to browse the menu, add items to their cart, and complete purchases from the comfort of their home.

  • Intended Users

    General public and existing customers of Wendy's Kitchen who want to conveniently browse and order food online without needing to call or visit in person.

  • Content

    The site will include a welcoming home page, a browsable food menu with item descriptions and prices, a shopping cart to review selections, a checkout page with payment options, and a contact form for customer inquiries

Site Map

Page Design

Home

  • Purpose of the page

    Landing Page of the site, this page must showcase information about the servises offered so customers might get interested in contracting the servises.

  • Audience/Users of page (customer, admin, etc.)

    General public and/or existing customers who might get interested on the information shown.

  • What will be content of the page?

    A home landing page, this page will showcase new dishes, promotions, or recent events that contracted the services being provided.

Menu

  • Purpose of the page

    The Menu page allows customers to browse all available food items offered by Wendy's Kitchen. It gives users the information they need to make ordering decisions and enables them to add items directly to their cart.

  • Audience/Users of page (customer, admin, etc.)

    This page is intended for customers who are actively looking to place an order.

  • What will be content of the page?

    The page will display a grid of food items, each shown as a card with an image, name, short description, and price. An "Add to Cart" button will be included on each item card. Items may be organized into categories such as mains, sides, and drinks.

Cart

  • Purpose of the page

    The Cart page gives customers a summary of all items they have selected before proceeding to checkout.

  • Audience/Users of page (customer, admin, etc.)

    This page is intendent to be used by customers who have added at least one item to their cart. It is a transitional step in the ordering process between browsing the menu and completing a purchase.

  • What will be content of the page?

    The page will list each selected item along with its name, quantity, and individual price. A running total will be displayed at the bottom, and buttons will be available to remove items or proceed to the checkout page.

Check-Out

  • Purpose of the page

    The Check-Out page is where customers complete their order by entering payment details and confirming their purchase.

  • Audience/Users of page (customer, admin, etc.)

    This page is used by customers who are ready to finalize their order.

  • What will be content of the page?

    The page will show the final order total and provide input fields for payment information such as card number, expiration date, CVV, and billing details. A submit button will allow customers to place their order, and a confirmation message will be displayed upon success.

Contact Us

  • Purpose of the page

    The Contact Us page provides customers with a way to reach out to Wendy's Kitchen directly with questions, feedback, or special requests.

  • Audience/Users of page (customer, admin, etc.)

    This page is available to all site visitors, including customers with order inquiries, those who want to provide feedback, or anyone seeking general information about Wendy's Kitchen.

  • What will be content of the page?

    The page will include a contact form with fields for the user's name, email address, subject, and message. A submit button will send the message to the client. Optionally, the page may also display business hours or an email address for direct contact.
